How it learns

Three things happen every time you cook something.

STEP 01

Photo → recipe

Qwen3-VL recognizes ingredients in your image and proposes a dish with prep steps and nutrition tailored to your current profile.

STEP 02

Remembers 14 days

Every meal is saved to your personal graph. The next generation includes a summary of your last two weeks so the AI avoids repeating dishes.

STEP 03

Profile evolves

A second AI pass reads your history and additively updates your preferences. The AI learned badge shows which ones came from patterns, not from a form.
